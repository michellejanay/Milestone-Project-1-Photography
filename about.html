About me

Hello and welcome to my static website. I am Michelle, the photographer behind all of these travel photographs. I am half English and half American, currently based in the UK. These photographs are a few of my favourites from my travels in the US, where I lived in a van and stepped foot in 40 out of 50 States.

Currently, I am working towards a Level 5 Diploma in Web Application Development. I will soon also be working my way to a Computer Science degree. I have experience as a Software Engineer, having worked with technologies such as Svelte and Google Chrome extensions. My favourite things about coding are continious learning, JavaScript, and the endless possibilities for creativity.
